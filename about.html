About Us

Who We Are

Welcome to Booze Cluez, your ultimate guide to navigating the vibrant and diverse bars of San Francisco! Founded by a group of friends with a passion for exploration and, of course, a good drink, we're here to make your bar-hopping experience organized, fun, and unforgettable.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to have one (1) drink at every bar, restaurant, or pub in San Francisco that serves alcohol. Why? Because life's too short to drink at the same bar twice! We're all about discovering new places, meeting new people, and enjoying the rich tapestry of experiences that San Francisco's nightlife has to offer.

History

Booze Cluez began as a labor of love, a strong desire to explore San Francisco, and just a tiny bit of an alcohol problem. It has been created to help you, an adult over 21 years of age and a lover of alcoholic beverages, to navigate the bars of San Francisco in an organized, fun and, dare we say, productive way.

How It Works

  1. Download the App: Get our Booze Cluez app from the App Store or Google Play or go to https://boozecluez.com.
  2. Choose Your Adventure: Browse through our curated list of bars, complete with ratings, reviews, and what to expect.
  3. Check-in and Cheers: Once you're at the bar, use the app to check-in, and then enjoy your drink!
  4. Share and Earn: Share your experience, rate the bar, and earn points that can be redeemed for exclusive Booze Cluez merchandise.

Why Join Us?

  • Organized Fun: We take the guesswork out of where to go next.
  • Community: Meet fellow adventurers who share your love for exploration and a good drink.
  • Exclusive Deals: Enjoy special offers and discounts at participating bars.
  • Booze Cluez Merch: Earn points and redeem them for exclusive Booze Cluez swag.

Responsible Drinking

We're all about having a good time, but we also take responsible drinking seriously. Always know your limits and ensure you have a safe way to get home.
